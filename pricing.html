Our Services

We offer pragmatic and state-of- the-art end to end sourcing and consulting solutions that enable organisations to manage their procurement activities effectively and efficiently helping them achieve a path of greater profitability. We deliver scalable and sustainable procurement solutions that perform for our customers as well as the world we live in.

"Higher profit margins with no loss in quality"

Manufacturers in our sourcing network play a large role in researching the new materials and techniques needed to develop tomorrow’s designs and products. Multi-tiered checking at the factory level, ensures quality control.

You are sourcing your products to streamline production costs and time while maintaining product quality. Most importantly, we can check the progress and quality of your project on-site as necessary. Our customers consistently find they are able to compete more effectively, improve profit margins, and raise the quality of their products above in-house products.

Our value-added service covers all aspects of turning great ideas into reality, supply solutions, pricing, logistics and delivery. As always, our goal is speed, quality and efficiency.

CARGO HANDLING

MONTAGU MARITIME PRIVATE LIMITED provides reliable and efficient cargo handling services. Whether your cargo is standard, oversized or requiring special attention, we make sure that it is packaged, loaded, unloaded and boarded appropriately and reach its destination safely. We provide services for oil & gas energy, mining, manufacturing, aerospace or any other industry.



SHIP CHARTERING

MONTAGU MARITIME PRIVATE LIMITED is a trusted name for chartering boats, barges and other conventional or non-conventional sea carriers. Our experienced team can avail you full or partial chartering services for import or export to worldwide destinations through a network of channel partners.


CUSTOMS CLEARANCE

MONTAGU MARITIME PRIVATE LIMITED is a leading and reliable agent. We have in-depth understanding of the entire customs process and formalities. Our services include clearing our clients through customs by maintaining complete documentation as per the compliance. Our team of experienced professionals has excellent acquaintance with customers officials and can help you fast track clearance of your goods. We ensure that there are no delays in movement of cargo and no cost overruns.


AIR FREIGHT

We provided best in class air freight forwarding services, combined with sea freight and surface transportation for import and export of a range of commercial cargo to the remotest parts of the world. We work with a number of standard carriers and alternate carriers to provide our clients flexibility in terms of delivery schedule, transportation time, services and rates. Whether consignment needs to be shipped immediately or under special conditions, we provide services for small, medium and large cargo of all types including electronics, machinery, automobiles, perishable goods, pharmaceutical goods and all other classes of goods.


SEA FREIGHT

We provide comprehensive, cost-effective and scalable sea-freight forwarding solutions for import and export of a wide variety of cargo with door-step delivery anywhere around the world. For multimodal transportation these services can be compounded with air freight and road transportation.


BREAK BULK

We provide break bulk shipping services for cargo, which is too big, over dimensional or unfit for shipping in containers such as construction equipment’s, cranes, big vehicles and large engines. We can negotiate space on heavy lift vessels and deliver your cargo in requisite time at minimum cost. We have experience, all the resources, capabilities and skills to safely transport such complex nature of goods to any inland and distant location.


PROJECT LOGISTICS

Often, complex projects require movement of project specific cargoes. At MONTAGU MARITIME PRIVATE LIMITED, we have state-of-the-art equipment’s and dedicated team of experts to quickly, safely and effectively mobilizing project cargo for diverse industries within India and across international destinations combining multi- model transport. We start with analysing your requirements, create a minute-detail strategy for transportation and implement the plan. We can deliver project specific cargo for the entire span of projects so that you can peacefully focus on the execution of your project.
